Quick Updates

  • Training a Spatio temporal Autoencoder that used 3D convolutions and Deconvolutions

  • The networks contains 1 encoder for representation learning and 2 decoder blocks for reconstruction of inputs and predicting the future sequence

  • Training with MSEloss but with a weighted variations for the prediction error

  • Datasets used in the paper are mostly generated from static (non moving camera) in fixed environment

  • When trained on the data collected on the Indian roads, the inputs are very dynamic along the objects in the enviroment the surrounding also change

  • Regularity score plot for test video sequence

  • The frames with less regularity score (anomalies) are lesser than the general population
  • By chosing the appropriate threshold, we classify the frames accordingly as anomaly or not

Results

  • Frames with Regularity score ranging from 0.9 to 1 (along with the regularity score around that frame)

  • Frames with Regularity score ranging from 0.8 to 0.9 (along with the regularity score around that frame)

  • Frames with Regularity score ranging from 0 to 0.1 (along with the regularity score around that frame)

Queries

  • Due to the changing surroundings, the network is learning represent and reconstruct road (which is more common than others) leading to trivial learning
  • Though the vehicles are reconstructed most of the times, but the error is dominated by the surrounding of the road
  • More data from the same route might help in better learning
